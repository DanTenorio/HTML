Projects

Morse Code App

My first app that was released on the play store was a morse code app. The purpose of the app was to train amateur HAM radio operator on dicephering call signs. My group worked closely with a professor at Augustana College who was an amateur HAM radio operator. We used Android Studio, Java, and Firebase to create the app.

College Souk App

After graduating, I decided to help maintain an app with another Augustana College alumnus. The College Souk App was created to assist students find used textbooks without going through Facebook or the bookstore. Initially, it started as a web app, but we found more success as we turned it into a phone app. Since then, it has found success on the iOS App Store and Google PlayStore. We used React, Java, and MongoDB to create the app. This introduced me to full stack development and all of its potential.
