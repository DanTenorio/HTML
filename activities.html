Extracurricular Activities

Music

I have been playing the trombone since I was 11. I joined my school's band when I was in 6th grade and have been playing since. I played in multiple bands in college including the Augustana Symphonic Band, Augustana Jazz Band, and the Augustana Concert Band. I enjoyed playing the trombone for many years and it has taught me a lot. It taught me practice isn't good enough, but perfect practice is. Also it gave me leadership opportunities as a section leader.

Martial Arts

I've been doing martial arts since I was 13. Martial arts is a pasttime I continue today along with my family.

Gardening

I recently picked up gardening as a hobby. It is a hobby of patience and consistency. The rewards reaped from gardening are delicous! My favorite plants to grow include tomatoes, cucumbers, and kohlrabi. A kohlrabi is radish-like vegetable and it's also known as a german radish.

Hiking and Camping and Fishing

I enjoy the great outdoors. Even though Illinois isn't known for the outdoors, there are quite a few camping areas with astounding natural beauty. I enjoy going for a hike, setting up a tent then going out to catch dinner at the nearest lake or river.
